1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how SMAJ Ecosystem ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website smaj.org (the "Site") and any related applications or services (collectively, the "Services").

This Cookie Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. By using our Site, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree, please adjust your browser settings to reject cookies or stop using our Site.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide information to website owners, and improve browsing experience.

Similar technologies include web beacons or pixels, local storage, SDKs, and fingerprinting techniques. For simplicity, this Cookie Policy uses the term "cookies" to refer to cookies and similar tracking technologies.

3. Why We Use Cookies

We may use cookies for essential site functionality, security, form behavior, analytics, performance improvement, remembering preferences, marketing measurement, and social media functionality where applicable.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

4.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function and cannot be disabled in our systems. They may be set in response to actions such as filling out forms, submitting applications, setting privacy preferences, or maintaining site security.

  • Session cookies: Used for session management and core site functionality.
  • Security cookies: Used to support security and prevent abuse.
  • Cookie consent storage: Used to remember cookie consent preferences where a cookie banner is enabled.

4.2. Analytics / Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Site by collecting and reporting information. They allow us to improve performance, content, and user experience. Where required by law, analytics cookies are used with consent.

Examples may include Google Analytics cookies such as _ga, _gid, and _gat, if analytics tools are active on the Site.

4.3. Marketing / Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be used to deliver ads relevant to your interests, limit how often you see ads, and measure campaign effectiveness. Where required by law, marketing cookies are used with consent.

Examples may include Meta/Facebook Pixel cookies such as _fbp or Google advertising cookies such as IDE, if these tools are active on the Site.

4.4. Social Media Cookies

These cookies may enable social sharing and track interactions with social media platforms such as LinkedIn, X, Instagram, YouTube, Telegram, TikTok, or other platforms linked from the Site. They may also be used by those platforms for advertising or analytics purposes.

4.5. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow the Site to remember choices you make, such as region, language, theme, or form preferences, to provide a more personalized experience.

5. First-Party vs. Third-Party Cookies

First-party cookies are set by our Site and can be read by our Site. Third-party cookies are set by domains other than smaj.org, such as analytics, advertising, embedded media, or social media providers. You can manage third-party cookies through cookie preferences where available or through your browser settings.

6. Session vs. Persistent Cookies

Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period, such as one year, and may be used for analytics, personalization, consent preferences, or advertising.

7. How to Manage Cookies

When cookie consent controls are available, you may accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or manage cookie preferences by category.

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, block third-party cookies, delete cookies after each session, or receive a notification when a cookie is set. If you block or delete essential cookies, some features of the Site may not function properly.

You may also opt out of interest-based advertising through industry tools such as the Network Advertising Initiative, Digital Advertising Alliance, or European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance.

8. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically.
