Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

SMAJ Ecosystem respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we may collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit our website, contact us, submit a partnership request, or interact with SMAJ Ecosystem online.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact SMAJ Ecosystem through our contact form
  • Submit a partnership, startup, or collaboration request
  • Subscribe to updates or newsletters
  • Communicate with us by email or social platforms
  • Show interest in SMAJ Ventures, SMAJ Labs, SMAJ Products, or SMAJ Partners

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to respond to inquiries, review partnership requests, evaluate collaboration opportunities, improve our website, communicate updates, and support SMAJ Ecosystem's company-building and partnership activities.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary with trusted service providers, technical tools, communication platforms, or partners involved in reviewing a collaboration request, subject to reasonable confidentiality and security practices.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. However, no online system is completely secure, and users should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal, financial, or confidential information unless officially requested through a secure channel.
